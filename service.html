Our Service Placements

Verbum Dei High School

An all-male Jesuit high school located in Watts, CA, members engage in tutoring and after school activities, ensuring that the student body maintains a 100% acceptance rate to colleges.

Safe Place For Youth

Safe Place for Youth is a shelter for youth experiencing the effects of Homelessness. Throughout the week, members serve at the Los Angeles placement by working directly with the youth.

Good Shepherd Shelter

Good Shepherd Shelter is a shelter for women and their children escaping domestic abuse. Members work with children through helping in the classroom, tutoring, and participating in after school activities.

El Espejo

Members participate in the El Espejo mentoring program at Lennox Middle School by providing mentorship to middle school students, tutoring, and participating in after school activities and field trips.

Blessed Sacrament Church

Blessed Sacrament Church is a parish in West Hollywood. Members serve in the food pantry and prepare and give out food to the community, helping to alleviate the effects of homelessness in the area.


Events

MAGIS Men are also active on campus. Every year, we organize advocacy events and fundraisers aimed at engaging the LMU community and educating each other about the social justice issues related to homelessness and education.



Homelessness Understanding Week


WHEN: Fall Semester

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

This week long event aims to examine the deeper issues of homelessness. Members engage in different activities for the rest of the LMU community to join in, including group reflections, service take-alongs to some of our placements, panel speakers, and nightly sleep outs on cardboard boxes on campus.

Better World Books Drive


WHEN: Finals Week semesterly

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

During finals week of every semester, we host our Better World Books Drive in front of the Bookstore, where we collect old books from students. Better World Books then uses and resells the books to fund education and literacy initiatives worldwide.

48 Hour


WHEN: Spring Semester

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

Every Spring Semester, we organize a 48-hour fundraiser to raise money for a financial aid package for a Verbum Dei High School student. The fundraiser also includes a social justice event that revolves around issues in the education system.
